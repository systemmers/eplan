


# For Instructors

## About ePlan
ePlan Inc. offers the corporate in-house teaching and other educational programs for qualified instructors since 2015. We are an organization of experts in each field and a company that provides customized learning services for students and instructors. Teachers who are novice in this industry also offer many opportunities to help their careers.

## Requirements
1. Bachelor's degree from a 4-year University
2. Native Speaker / Fluent speaker of the language to be taught
3. Legally allowed to stay and work in Korea
4. Teaching certificate(TESOL, TEFL, etc.)
